The Pacific Masters Open Water Challenge recognizes our members' accumulated Open Water swims!

How does it work? For every Pacific Masters sanctioned open water swim that a Pacific Masters swimmer successfully completes beginning with the 2021 season, that swimmer will receive credit for one swim. Complete 5 total open water swims successfully, and the swimmer earns "Trout" status. The swimmer will elevate to "Manatee" with the 10th successfully completed swim, and so on.
Guidelines:
  • A swimmer can compete in both Suit category 1 and 2. All Open Water swims ("wetsuit" and "non-wetsuit") count for this challenge.
  • All successfully completed sanctioned swims longer than 500m count.
  • When swimming at a festival (for example, Lake Berryessa, Del Valle, Santa Cruz, or Keller Cove) each event swum counts as one swim.
Hints:
  • Within each different status (Trout, Manatee, etc.) swimmers are ordered first by number of swims, and then by total distance swum.
  • Want to see more details about a swimmer's OW accomplishments? Just click on their name.
  • Want to see more details about a particular year's Open Water swims for a specific swimmer? After clicking on their name select the suit category swims desired (either Cat 1 or Cat 2, depending on which suit categories they swam in).
